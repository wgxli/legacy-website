Past Teaching Aids

In Fall 2017, I taught several lessons to an IB Mathematics HL class in collaboration with my high school math teacher. Below are some of the teaching aids I created during this experience.

  • Mathematical Induction (PDF) — Overview of induction.
  • Common Graphs (PDF) — An overview of exponential, logarithmic, and rational functions. Touches upon the visualization of complex exponentials and the structure of the Möbius group.
  • Trigonometric Identities — Derivation of multiple-, sum-, and half-angle formulas in three different ways. Features an interactive diagram and some higher-level examples.
  • Set Theory — Introduction to set theory. Features two interactive diagrams, challenging exercises, and a philosophical discussion.
